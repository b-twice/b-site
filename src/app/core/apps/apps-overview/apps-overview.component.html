Grocery List arrow_forward

A light-weight checklist of groceries needed for the week.

Developed out of a need to stop the tedium of writing out ingredients by hand. The list works by taking recipes, which have ingredients, and adding them to a meal plan. The meal plan then combines all the ingredients into a list of groceries for the week.

Stack: Angular

Launched October 2018

Budgeting arrow_forward

A comprehensive, multi-purpose budgeting app turned partial life app.

Track budgets, transactions, recipes, meal plans, groceries, books, and food products. Visualize changes by year with embedded graphs. Get a month break down of budgets. All transactions are imported from financial instutions in an ETL. All food related data entered manually through receipts and cookbooks using CRUD workflows in the web app.

Stack: Angular | .NET Core | SQLite

Launched April 2017

Richmond Ultimate Scores arrow_forward

A simple score page for viewing league scores and team results by year.

I participated in a local Ultimate Frisbee league for a few years. While playing, I creating this app as a fun way for teams to see their scores. Unfortunately, I have stopped playing and the site is no longer updated.

Stack: Angular | Python Flask | SQLite

Launched May 2016
